## 💡 Tips

1. **Keep it simple** - One clear donate button is better than 5 options
2. **Be genuine** - People support people, not just projects
3. **Show progress** - Post updates about what donations helped achieve
4. **Say thank you** - Publicly thank supporters (with permission)
5. **Don't be pushy** - Make it available but not annoying
